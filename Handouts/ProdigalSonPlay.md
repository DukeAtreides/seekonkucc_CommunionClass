# Short Skit Script: The Prodigal Son

**Roles:**
- Narrator
- Father
- Younger Son
- Older Brother
- Servants (optional)

---

### Scene 1: Leaving Home

**Narrator:**  
There was a man who had two sons. One day, the younger son said to his father,  
**Younger Son:**  
“Father, please give me my share of the family money now.”  

**Father:**  
(looks sad but gives money)  
“Be careful, my son.”  

**Narrator:**  
The younger son took his money and left home.

---

### Scene 2: Wasting Money

**Narrator:**  
The younger son spent all his money on parties and fun. Soon, he had nothing left.  

**Younger Son:**  
(sad, hungry)  
“I am so hungry. Even the servants have more food than me.”  

---

### Scene 3: Deciding to Return

**Narrator:**  
The younger son thought,  
**Younger Son:**  
“I will go back to my father and say, ‘I am sorry. Please let me be your servant.’”  

---

### Scene 4: Father’s Welcome

**Narrator:**  
While the son was still far away, his father saw him and ran to him.  

**Father:**  
(runs and hugs son)  
“My son! You are home! Let’s celebrate!”  

---

### Scene 5: Older Brother’s Reaction

**Narrator:**  
The older brother came home and was angry.  

**Older Brother:**  
“Why are you celebrating him? He wasted everything!”  

**Father:**  
“Your brother was lost and now is found. We must celebrate!”  

---

### End

**Narrator:**  
And that is the story of the Prodigal Son, a story about forgiveness and love.
