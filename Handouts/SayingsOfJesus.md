## These are some sayings from the Gospels and the Acts of the Apostles.
- The Gospels are stories, biographies, of Jesus' life. There are 4: Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John.
- Acts of the Apostles is a continuation of the Gospel of Luke.

| Saying | Gospel Reference | Core Teaching |
| -- | -- | -- |
| "Where your treasure is there your heart will be also." |	Matthew 6:21 |	Priorities reveal the heart |
| "You cannot serve both God and money." |	Matthew 6:24; Luke 16:13 | Loyalty to God over wealth |
| "It is more blessed to give than to receive." |	Acts 20:35 |	Generosity |
| "The first shall be last, and the last shall be first." |	Matthew 19:30; Mark 10:31	| Humility and reversal of status |
| "Whoever wants to be great must be servant." |	Matthew 20:26	| Servant leadership |
| "Blessed are the meek for they shall inherit the earth" |	Matthew 5:5	| Humility rewarded |
| "Let the little children come to me."	| Matthew 19:14	| Value of childlike faith |
| "Love your neighbor as yourself."	| Matthew 22:39; Luke 10:27	| Compassion and love |
| "Do to others as you would have them do to you." |	Luke 6:31 |	Ethical reciprocity |
| "Forgive, and you will be forgiven."	| Luke 6:37 |	Importance of forgiveness |
| "Peace I leave with you." |	John 14:27	| Spiritual peace |
| "Take up your cross and follow me." |	Matthew 16:24	| Discipleship and sacrifice |
