## Keywords 

| Keyword | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Love	| Caring about someone a lot and wanting to be kind to them. |
| Compassion | Feeling sorry when someone is sad or hurt and wanting to help them. |
| Empathy	| Understanding how someone else feels, like if they are happy or sad. |
| Golden Rule |  Treat others the way you want them to treat you. |
| Forgiveness	| Saying it’s okay when someone makes a mistake and not being mad at them anymore. |
| Mercy	| (definition 1) Being kind and not punishing someone even if they did something wrong.  |
| Mercy | (definition 2) Doing things to help people in need, like feeding the hungry or welcoming strangers.|
| Repentance | Saying sorry for something bad you did and trying to do better next time.|
| Humility	| Being humble means not bragging and knowing it’s okay to ask for help. |
| Sacrifice |	Giving up something you like to help someone else or do the right thing.|
| Community |	A group of people who live or work together and help each other.|

## Examples

"If you want your friends to be nice to you, then you should be nice to them and try to understand how they feel, just like the Golden Rule says."
This shows how empathy (understanding feelings) and the Golden Rule (treat others how you want to be treated) go together.

"If you want new friends to feel happy and welcome in your community, you should be kind to them and try to understand how they feel, just like the Golden Rule teaches."
